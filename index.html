Biography

I am a third-year M.S. student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanning Normal University, affiliated with the Guangxi Key Lab of Human-machine Interaction and Intelligent Decision. I'm honored to be advised by Prof. Yuzhong Peng.

My research interests include structure-based drug design, 3D molecular generation, deep learning for molecular property prediction, and geometric deep learning on protein-ligand complexes. I am also passionate about diffusion models, transformers, and graph neural networks for molecular modeling and analysis.
