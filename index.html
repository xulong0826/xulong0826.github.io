Biography

I am a third-year M.S. student in the Guangxi Key Lab of Human-machine Interaction and Intelligent Decision at Nanning Normal University, under the supervision of Prof. Yuzhong Peng.

My research interests focus on structure-based drug design, 3D molecular generation, and deep learning for molecular property prediction. I am also passionate about geometric deep learning on protein-ligand complexes, as well as the development and application of diffusion models, transformers, and graph neural networks for molecular modeling and analysis.
